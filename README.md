An end-to-end **Data Analytics Portfolio Project** focused on understanding customer shopping behavior, identifying purchasing trends, and delivering business recommendations using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

A leading retail company wanted to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve:

* Sales performance
* Customer satisfaction
* Long-term loyalty
* Product and marketing strategies

This project analyzes **3,900 customer transactions** across multiple product categories to uncover insights into:

* customer segments
* revenue trends
* discount behavior
* subscription patterns
* product preferences

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

**How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?**

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Python** → Pandas, NumPy, Data Cleaning, Feature Engineering
* **SQL (MySQL / PostgreSQL)** → Business queries, segmentation, KPI analysis
* **Power BI** → Interactive dashboard, KPIs, slicers, trend visualization
* **Jupyter Notebook**
* **GitHub**

---

## 📂 Dataset Summary

* **Rows:** 3,900
* **Columns:** 18
* **Missing Values:** 37 in review_rating
* **Key Features:**

  * Customer demographics
  * purchase amount
  * category
  * discount applied
  * subscription status
  * shipping type
  * review rating
  * previous purchases

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering

Performed using **Python**:

* handled missing values using median imputation
* standardized column names to snake_case
* created `age_group`
* created customer segmentation features
* removed redundant columns
* loaded cleaned dataset into SQL database

---

## 📊 SQL Business Analysis

Solved 10 key business problems:

1. Revenue by gender
2. High-spending discount users
3. Top 5 products by average rating
4. Shipping type comparison
5. Subscriber vs non-subscriber spend
6. Discount-dependent products
7. Customer segmentation
8. Top 3 products per category
9. Repeat buyers vs subscriptions
10. Revenue by age group

---

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard

Built an interactive dashboard with:

* KPI cards
* revenue trends
* sales by category
* customer segments
* subscription insights
* age group analysis
* interactive slicers

Dashboard KPIs:

* **3.9K customers**
* **$59.76 average purchase amount**
* **3.75 average review rating**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Male customers generated significantly higher revenue
* Loyal customers formed the largest customer segment
* Clothing category contributed highest revenue
* Discount-driven products showed strong sales dependency
* Young adults generated the highest revenue contribution

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

* Boost subscription benefits
* Launch loyalty programs
* Optimize discount strategy
* Focus marketing on high-revenue age groups
* Promote top-rated products
